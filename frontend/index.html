Who We Are

A youth-led enterprise rooted in rural Rwanda

AYIKB is a youth-led agribusiness enterprise based in the Kirehe District of Rwanda's Eastern Province. We focus on sustainable rural development and empowering young people through modern farming.

By combining crop cultivation with livestock rearing, we maximise resource efficiency and move beyond traditional subsistence farming — creating real jobs and lasting economic growth for our community.

  • Sustainable rural development
  • Empowering young Rwandans
  • Modern agricultural techniques
  • Climate resilience for the region
