The Beginning

A vision for youth in agriculture

Young people in Kirehe recognised the need to move beyond subsistence farming and create real opportunities in agribusiness.

Founding

AYIKB is established

Agri Youth Innovation Kirehe Business Limited is founded as a youth-led enterprise focused on sustainable rural development.

Growth

Mixed farming integration

We combined crop cultivation with livestock rearing and introduced a circular resource system using organic manure.

Today

Strengthening the value chain

We work directly within local agricultural channels, creating jobs and safeguarding food security across the region.

Scaling impact

We aim to expand our reach, adopt more technology, and boost climate resilience throughout the Kirehe region.
