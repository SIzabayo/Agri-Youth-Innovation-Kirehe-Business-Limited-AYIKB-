About AYIKB

A youth-led agribusiness enterprise driving sustainable rural development in Kirehe District, Rwanda.

Who We Are

Empowering young people through modern farming

Agri Youth Innovation Kirehe Business Limited (AYIKB) is a youth-led agribusiness enterprise based in the Kirehe District of Rwanda's Eastern Province. We focus on sustainable rural development and empowering young people through modern farming.

We combine crop cultivation with livestock rearing to maximise resource efficiency, using organic livestock manure to enrich our fields and maintain soil fertility. By working directly within local agricultural channels, we improve productivity and help safeguard food security in our region.

  • Sustainable rural development
  • Empowering young Rwandans
  • Modern agricultural techniques
  • Climate resilience for the region
